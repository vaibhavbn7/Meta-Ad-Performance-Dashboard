# Meta Ad Performance Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project analyzes Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising campaign performance using Power BI. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of campaign reach, engagement, conversions, audience demographics, geographic distribution, and ad format effectiveness. The goal is to help marketing teams optimize campaign performance and maximize return on advertising spend.

## Problem Statement

Digital marketing campaigns generate large volumes of performance data across multiple channels, making it difficult for marketers to identify which campaigns, audiences, and ad formats deliver the best results.

The objective of this project was to:

- Monitor key advertising KPIs such as Impressions, Clicks, Engagements, CTR, Conversion Rate, and Purchases.
- Analyze audience behavior based on age, gender, and geographic location.
- Identify the most effective ad formats and campaign types.
- Track engagement patterns across different time periods.
- Support data-driven budget allocation and campaign optimization.
- Provide a centralized dashboard for marketing performance analysis.

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive KPI cards for campaign performance tracking.
- Dynamic filtering by Campaign Name and Target Interest.
- Audience demographic analysis (Age & Gender).
- Geographic engagement analysis using map visualizations.
- Weekly and hourly engagement trend analysis.
- Ad format performance comparison (Carousel, Image, Stories, Video).
- Campaign calendar view for performance monitoring.
- Dynamic measure selection for flexible analysis.
## Key Insights
## Campaign Performance
- Achieved over 339K impressions, 40K clicks, and 46K engagements, indicating strong campaign visibility and audience interaction.
- CTR reached approximately 11.8%, significantly higher than typical industry benchmarks, demonstrating effective ad creatives and targeting.
- Conversion Rate exceeded 5%, showing healthy conversion performance from engaged users.
## Audience Analysis
- Female audiences generated the highest engagement compared to other demographic groups.
- Users aged 18–30 years represented the most active and engaged audience segment.
- Engagement declined significantly for audiences above 35 years of age.
## Geographic Analysis
- Highest engagement originated from regions such as the United States, India, Brazil, Germany, and the United Kingdom.
- Emerging markets showed strong engagement potential for future campaign expansion.
## Time-Based Analysis
- Engagement remained relatively stable throughout the campaign period.
- Peak engagement occurred during afternoon and evening hours, indicating optimal ad delivery windows.
- Specific campaign dates showed significantly higher activity, suggesting successful promotional events or campaign launches.
## Ad Format Performance
- Video Ads delivered the highest CTR, Engagement Rate, and Conversion Rate.
- Stories Ads generated the highest impression volume and strong engagement performance.
- Carousel and Image ads performed consistently but delivered slightly lower conversion rates.
## Business Recommendations
- Increase budget allocation toward Video and Stories campaigns.
- Prioritize targeting females aged 18–30 for improved campaign ROI.
- Schedule campaigns during afternoon and evening peak engagement periods.
- Optimize landing pages and retargeting strategies to improve purchase rates.
- Focus investments on high-performing geographic regions and audience segments.
## Business Impact
- Enabled real-time monitoring of marketing campaign performance.
- Improved visibility into audience behavior and engagement trends.
- Supported data-driven advertising decisions and budget optimization.
- Helped identify high-performing ad formats, demographics, and campaign strategies to improve marketing effectiveness.
